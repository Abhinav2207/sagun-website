Welcome to

Sagun Proteins Pvt. Ltd

Sagun Proteins Pvt. Ltd. is known to be the biggest manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Superior quality Industrial Grade Acid Casein and Edible Grade Acid Casein in INDIA. We have started Casein Business in 1999. We offer the variety of Casein in different grade to meet the requirement of each and every customer according to the market demand. We are fully equipped with advanced plant & machinery to carry out the quality products as per the buyer’s requirements. We manufacture our products under the constant observation of our technical professional team to ensure the best and consistent quality throughout the year.
We are the fastest growing Casein Company having experience more than 15 Years. In this very short span of time we have achieved very good market share throughout of country. We deliver superior quality casein products in domestic as well as in international market. Our plant is situated in Madhya Pradesh, central part of India that is very milk rich belt and ensure round the clock connectivity to any part of India. It makes our product qualitative and easy for fast delivery.

About us

SAGUN is the renowned name in the field of Acid Casein. We are the biggest manufacturer and supplier of Industrial Grade Acid Casein and Edible Grade Acid Casein in India. Established in 1935, we commenced things with different milk products. Later on in 1999, we put steps in the Casein Manufacturing sector under the name Sagun Food & Dairy Products with the vision to lead market by serving it with the supreme quality of Casein.

With the skills of the industry and motto to lead the business we have attained a top-niche in both domestic and international markets, as a Manufacturer, Supplier and Exporter of wide array of products. Our products are widely used in various industries such as Safety Match, Paint, Chemical, Leather, Paper, Textiles, Plastics and many more.
