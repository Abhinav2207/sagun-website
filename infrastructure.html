INFRASTRUCTURE

Our sound and advance infrastructural base is the pillar of our company and has contributed to its faster growth. Our unit is supported with the State-of-the-art machinery for manufacturing the quality product.
We have a huge plant base in Morena, equipped with advanced generation of machinery like Alfa Laval Decanter, Alfa Laval Separator, Thermax Boiler, Ring Dryer, Pulveriser, Mixer, Grinder etc. to manufacture quality products. Our plant is situated in a vast area and with strength of more than 150 employees. We possess the capability to deliver big requirements on time.
We also have an excellent premises facility for advanced packaging and storage to keep the final product in good and safe condition. We are currently equipped with the capacity to manufacture 1500 MT Casein per annum which meets all the required standards.
Quality Policy
We believe in ensuring supreme quality of the products. We manufacture Casein using quality tested raw material obtained from authentic Vendors. We always deliver hassle-free products to our esteemed customers. In Addition to this, all of our products are tested on various quality parameters that further guarantee our credibility.
We also have a fully equipped in house laboratory to carry out all the tests related to Casein according to the industry standard parameters that ensures the perfect quality of the product to be served to different industries.
